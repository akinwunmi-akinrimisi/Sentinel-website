## 4. POSITIONING & COPY DIRECTION

**Unique Mechanism:**
The Sentinel Certification System™

A three-phase methodology used on every contract:

Phase 1 — Precision Gap Assessment
Before any training begins, every team member completes a structured diagnostic.
We map individual knowledge gaps against the exact CompTIA exam objectives.
No one sits through content they already know. No one skips content they need.

Phase 2 — Instructor-Led Certification Training
Live, structured sessions led by certified instructors (CISSP, CASP+, CySA+ credentialed).
Content mapped 1:1 to CompTIA exam domains. No generic cybersecurity theory.
Sessions conducted via Microsoft Teams or Zoom. Client selects schedule.
Typical cadence: 3x weekly sessions, 2 hours each.

Phase 3 — Exam Simulation and Readiness Certification
600+ practice questions in CompTIA exam format. Timed mock exams under real conditions.
Weekly performance tracking shared with the CISO or training lead.
No team member sits the live exam until they are consistently scoring above 85%.
Readiness sign-off issued per individual before exam scheduling.

Result: 96% first-attempt pass rate across all programs. Documented. Reportable.
